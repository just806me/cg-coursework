Google Chrome Experiments was launched in March 2009 with 19 experiments as a demonstration and test of Google's V8 JavaScript engine. The earliest projects featured on the site were mainly visualizations, interactive toys, and simple online games.
The website is steadily gaining popularity along with the number of featured experiments. It took the role of showcasing and experimenting latest open-source web-based technologies.
The number of experiments increased to 100 by July 2010.
By September 2012 number of exhibits on the website was up to 500. And by 2015, number of different Chrome projects posted on the website exceeded 1000.
As of March 2019, there were more than 1300 different Chrome experiments. The resourse also started to feature collection of experiments for Virtual and Augmented Reality, Robotics, AI, Creatability, Art and other projects.
