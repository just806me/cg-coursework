Google Chrome Experiments was launched in March 2009 with 19 experiments as a demonstration and test of Google's V8 JavaScript engine. The earliest projects featured on the site were mainly visualizations, interactive toys, and simple online games.
2009
The website is steadily gaining popularity along with the number of featured experiments. It took the role of showcasing and experimenting latest open-source web-based technologies. The number of experiments increased to 100 by July 2010.
